Instructions

You will be randomly and anonymously grouped with 4 other participants. Each of you will be asked to pick a number between 0 and 100. The winner will be the participant whose number is closest to two-third of the average of all chosen numbers.

The winner will receive 100 points, while other participants will receive nothing. In case of a tie, the 100 points will be equally divided between winners.

For your convenience, these instructions will remain available to you on all subsequent screens of this study.
